RAVE is the first neural synthesizer with high-quality (48 kHz) audio and 20x faster than real time on a laptop CPU, bringing artificial intelligence out of the lab and into performance. This allows timbre transfer, morphing, and resynthesis inside Ableton, Max/MSP, Pure Data.

It supports custom model training and can be used through standalone tools, plugins, Max/MSP, Pure Data, and web demos.
