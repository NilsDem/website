Gaîté Lyrique & IRCAM Forum Session Studio

Neuroscan is a collaborative project between French electronic music producer Canblaster and visual artist Neurotypique, notably developed through a residency with the ACIDS team at IRCAM.

The performance builds on generative models derived from AFTER and RAVE, trained on Canblaster's own musical material. These models enable personalized timbre transfers across his discography, as well as controllable, time-synced breakbeat generation driven by a MIDI clock. On stage, Canblaster performs with AFTER in real time, using it to shape melodic choir lines and hybrid lead synthesizer textures.

Neurotypique extends these model dynamics into the visual domain, translating AI parameters and latent controls into reactive visual architectures. The result is both an immersive live performance and a pedagogical exposition of the generative systems at play.
