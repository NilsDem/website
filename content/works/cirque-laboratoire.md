Cirque Laboratoire - Cirque Electrique, Paris

Event: Cirque Laboratoire experimental performance series

Description: Art-science live act blending performance, sensors, AI (RAVE, nn~). A two-day fusion of circus, AI-driven music, and performance where the big-top becomes a live instrument. Featuring ACIDS and IRCAM, Philippe Esling performed alongside trapeze artists, motion-capture dancers, and RAVE/neural synthesis systems for an immersive art-science encounter.
