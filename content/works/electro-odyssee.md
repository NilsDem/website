Molécule is a french composer and producer known for crafting immersive electronic works shaped by extreme environments and field recordings.

Presented at IRCAM, this performance leverages that AFTER and RAVE transformative potential to reinterprets his orchestral work (Symphonie n°1 « Quantique ») through modular textures and generated choirs.

The performance also explores the controllable transformation of field recordings - wind, ocean, and environmental sounds captured during Molécule's expeditions - into musical and rhythmic matter, shaping the narrative arc of the piece through generative processes and spatialized sound.

A first performance was presented during IRCAM Forum Session studio before the final concert at Enghien-les-bains in March 2026.
