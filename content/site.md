I am a researcher and developer exploring the potential of machine learning for musical creation. I recently completed my PHD at Ircam within the ACIDS research group. 

My work focuses on building neural audio instruments that foster new forms of interaction and open up new sonic spaces. Designed for real-time use, these systems are developed through close collaborations with artists and are used in live performances, installations, and musical compositions.
