I am a researcher and artist working at the intersection of machine learning, audio synthesis, and musical creation.

My work focuses on creative audio technologies, neural sound models, and collaborations with artists who use these systems in live performance, composition, and experimental media.

This website gathers research papers, software repositories, artistic projects, media documentation, and teaching material.
