# IRRRI - Pierce Warnecke & Matthew Biederman

IRRRI is a speculative audiovisual archaeology of our present, imagined from a distant future in which researchers sift through the debris of our media sphere to understand the collapse of our society.

Presented at the IRCAM Forum Session Studio, the performance explores AI as both lens and instrument - a tool for distortion, reinterpretation, and reconstruction. Sound is transformed using RAVE, while Torchbend enables the real-time bending of image-generation networks, producing unstable and shifting visual landscapes.

The project also marks the first live performance with the JUNK hardware synthesizer running custom rave models.
