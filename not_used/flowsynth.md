---
title: FlowSynth
type: tool
status: research
summary: A system for mapping synthesizer parameters and latent audio spaces.
links:
  demo: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UufQwUitBIw
---

FlowSynth learns an invertible mapping between the latent space of audio capabilities of a synthesizer and the space of its parameters. This enables to infer synthesizer parameters from an audio sample, or to control the synthesizer with voice or movement by navigating an organised manifold its sonic possibilities.
